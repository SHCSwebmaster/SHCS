Giving to SHCS

Thank you for making a donation to Sacred Heart Cathedral School. Clicking the appropriate Donate button below will take you to PayPal, where your donation will be securely processed. You can pay using your PayPal account or your credit card.

If you are making your donation in memory or honor of someone special, there will be an option when you complete your payment that states Honoree. Please select this and enter the name of the person that you wish to remember. We will also, at your request, notify the person or their family, but in order to do so, we need you to contact us with their current mailing address. You can do this either by phone or via e-mail.
